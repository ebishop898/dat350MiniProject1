# Lighting Preference Survey

by *Emily Bishop*

This project presents the design, distribution, and analysis of a survey focused on **lighting preferences and perceptions**. The survey was created to explore how individuals experience and interpret lighting in everyday environments and how those preferences may vary across respondents.

The project emphasizes survey design, data collection, and reflection on results, with a focus on connecting real-world experiences to quantitative and qualitative analysis.
